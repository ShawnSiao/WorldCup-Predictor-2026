## Source Policy

Primary sources take priority over secondary sources.

Primary sources:

- FIFA schedule, fixtures, stadiums, results, squads, and rankings.
- Official federation announcements.
- Official venue or match pages.

Secondary sources may be used only when they add context that FIFA does not publish directly, such as injuries, expected lineups, tactical context, or post-match statistics.
